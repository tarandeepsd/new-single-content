Why register on Shout

Shout can be consulted without registration, by the way, we recommend you to register. Why?

  • Registration in FREE!
  • By signing up you will have access to our exclusive features: the My Feed section, a completely customized Newsletter based on your preferences, special recommendations about the best time to buy the products you like (learn more).
  • Registered Shouters can take part to the Affiliate Program, that gives you the opportunity to earn an extra income (learn more)

How to register on Shout

Registration is quick and easy, all you need is a valid email address. Follow these four steps:

  • Open the Sign Up pop up by clicking on the Sign Up button;
  • Enter your email and choose a password. Alternatively, you can register using one of your Social Network accounts: Facebook, Twitter or Google+. Just click on one of the Social buttons.
